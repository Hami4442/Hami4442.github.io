About

Senior video editor with 15+ years of experience as an independent editor and former business owner, delivering broadcast-ready content for top-tier networks. Specializes in fast-turnaround reality, documentary, unscripted & scripted editing and sports with a strong focus on pacing, tone consistency, and story clarity under tight deadlines.

Skilled in AVID, Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and Photoshop. Adept at visual storytelling, problem solving, maintaining consistent tone, and delivering polished content under tight deadlines. Proficient in a full range of post-production workflows.
